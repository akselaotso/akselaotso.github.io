This is Delta Attack

Delta Attack is a Helsinki, Finland based blues duo composed of Jani Dilen (guitar & stompbox) and Matti Aksela (vocals & harmonica). Please do not hesitate to reach out for bookings or just to talk blues!

We operate within Helsinki, Kirkkonummi, Vantaa and Espoo with a number of performances under our belt.
